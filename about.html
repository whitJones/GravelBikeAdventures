About Us

Our Mission

Gravel Bike Adventures was started by Whit, a mountain biking enthusiasts in Denver, CO with a sense of adventure and a desire to be inclusive to all. I believe it is vitally important to include women's voices in the gravel and mountain biking industry. My goal is to encourage all people (especially women and members of the LGBTQ+ community) to explore the power of the outdoors through venturing onto dirt roads. Our interactive website provides routes, downloadable maps, advice and other resources.
